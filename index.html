Installation & FAQ

Everything you need to know to get started with compX.

How do I install compX? (.zxp Guide)

Step 1: Download a ZXP Installer (like aescripts + aeplugins manager or ZXP Installer).

Step 2: Completely close Adobe After Effects.

Step 3: Open the installer and drag & drop the compX.zxp file into it.

Step 4: Re-open After Effects and navigate to Window → Extensions → compX.

Which versions of After Effects are supported?

compX supports After Effects 2021 and above on both Windows and macOS.

Can I use it on multiple machines?

Each license key allows activation on one device only. To use compX on another machine, you will need an additional license.

🔑 License Activation & Support

Your license is tied to your email address and is device-bound. On first launch, simply enter your licensed email and click Activate — an internet connection is needed only for this step.

You can freely deactivate your license from the plugin (logout button in the header) and re-activate on a different machine whenever needed.
